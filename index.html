As you could have probably guessed, my name is Shubham Kumar Jain, currently a graduate student in Master of Science, Computer and Information Sciences at Stony Brook University, New York, US. When my 8th grade teacher swapped the face of Mona Lisa with that of donald duck, I realised the power of Photoshop. Soon after, what fascinated me was that one animated car wiggling back and forth on a webpage. Coming back to home and recreating the same on my computer screen was the first milestone that I achieved.

It was in the Spring of 2016 when I was implementing on one of my project, D.A.R.T.S on python, I fell in love with the simplicity and beauty of that language. Enlivened to blend graphics into it, I typed "Visualization in Python" on the Google search bar. Five minutes of fiddling around the links was all it took for me to be mesmerized by the world of Data Science and that was the time when I decided to pursue my Master's along the same lines.

Since then, I had taken up various courses & MOOCs for Machine Learning, Data Science Fundamentals, and many more. I have also developed a project named, Stock Market Price Analysis which analyses and visualizes large datasets to critically evaluate the financial markets. As they say, Data is the new oil in this world of pixels.
